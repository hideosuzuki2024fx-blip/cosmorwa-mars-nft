# Mars NFT Land: Licensing and Advisory Agreement Template

## INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY LICENSING AGREEMENT

**This Agreement is entered into as of [DATE] between:**

**LICENSOR:** [IDEA PROVIDER NAME] ("Licensor")

**LICENSEE:** Space Exploration Technologies Corp. (SpaceX) ("Licensee")

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

**"Mars NFT Land Concept"** means the complete intellectual property, business model, and technical framework for creating a community engagement platform based on Mars land ownership through NFTs, including but not limited to:
- The core concept of tokenizing Mars parcels as NFTs
- The event-driven value model (discoveries, landings, geological events)
- The technical architecture for oracle integration and data feeds
- The business model for revenue generation (primary sales, secondary royalties, data licensing, subscriptions)
- All documentation, specifications, and strategic positioning materials

**"Licensed Technology"** means all software, smart contracts, and technical implementations derived from the Mars NFT Land Concept, including but not limited to ERC-721 NFT contracts, oracle integration, and data feed systems.

**"Gross Revenue"** means all revenue received by Licensee from the Mars NFT Land platform, including primary NFT sales, secondary market royalties, data licensing fees, subscription revenue, and any other monetization streams, before deduction of platform operating costs, but after deduction of payment processing fees (not to exceed 3%).

**"Confidential Information"** means all non-public information related to the Mars NFT Land Concept, including business plans, financial projections, user data, and strategic partnerships.

---

## 2. GRANT OF LICENSE

### 2.1 Exclusive License

Licensor hereby grants to Licensee an **exclusive, worldwide, royalty-bearing license** to develop, deploy, and commercialize the Mars NFT Land Concept for space exploration applications, including but not limited to:
- Mars exploration and colonization initiatives
- Starship mission engagement
- Lunar exploration applications
- Other celestial body exploration (with Licensor consent)

### 2.2 Restrictions

Licensee may not sublicense, transfer, or assign this license without prior written consent from Licensor, except to wholly-owned subsidiaries or with Licensor's explicit approval.

Licensor retains the right to use the Mars NFT Land Concept for non-space applications (e.g., Earth-based real estate, virtual worlds) outside the scope of this license.

---

## 3. FINANCIAL TERMS

### 3.1 Upfront Licensing Fee

Licensee shall pay Licensor a one-time licensing fee of **$3,000,000 USD** within 30 days of agreement execution. This fee represents payment for the intellectual property, business model, and strategic positioning of the Mars NFT Land Concept.

### 3.2 Ongoing Royalty

Licensee shall pay Licensor a **3% royalty on Gross Revenue** from the Mars NFT Land platform, calculated and paid quarterly as follows:

- **Q1 2026:** First royalty payment due April 30, 2026
- **Q2 2026:** Due July 31, 2026
- **Q3 2026:** Due October 31, 2026
- **Q4 2026:** Due January 31, 2027
- Subsequent years: Quarterly payments due 30 days after quarter end

### 3.3 Minimum Royalty

If Gross Revenue in any calendar year is less than $50M, Licensee shall pay a minimum royalty of $1.5M for that year. This minimum ensures Licensor receives meaningful compensation even during slower growth periods.

### 3.4 Revenue Sharing Example

| Year | Gross Revenue | Royalty (3%) | Minimum Royalty | Amount Due |
|------|---------------|--------------|-----------------|------------|
| 2026 | $150M | $4.5M | $1.5M | $4.5M |
| 2027 | $380M | $11.4M | $1.5M | $11.4M |
| 2028 | $570M | $17.1M | $1.5M | $17.1M |
| **3-Year Total** | **$1.1B** | **$33M** | — | **$33M** |

### 3.5 Payment Terms

All royalty payments shall be made via wire transfer to Licensor's designated bank account. Licensee shall provide detailed accounting statements with each royalty payment, showing:
- Total Gross Revenue for the period
- Breakdown by revenue stream (primary sales, secondary royalties, data licensing, subscriptions)
- Calculation of royalty amount
- Payment processing fees deducted

## 4. AUDIT RIGHTS

### 4.1 Annual Audit

Licensor has the right to audit Licensee's books and records related to Mars NFT Land revenue once per calendar year. Audits shall be conducted by an independent third-party accounting firm mutually agreed upon by both parties.

### 4.2 Audit Procedures

Licensee shall provide reasonable access to its financial records within 15 days of audit request. If an audit reveals underpayment of more than 5%, Licensee shall reimburse Licensor for the audit costs plus the underpaid royalties plus 10% interest.

### 4.3 Confidentiality of Audit

All audit findings shall remain confidential between Licensor, Licensee, and the auditing firm.

---

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY OWNERSHIP

### 5.1 Original Concept Ownership

Licensor retains all ownership rights to the Mars NFT Land Concept as originally created. This includes all strategic positioning, business model design, and conceptual frameworks.

### 5.2 Derivative Works

Licensee owns all derivative works, including smart contracts, user interfaces, marketing materials, and technical implementations created by Licensee during the term of this agreement. However, Licensee grants Licensor a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use these derivative works for advisory and consulting purposes.

### 5.3 Patent Rights

If either party develops patentable technology related to Mars NFT Land, the party developing such technology shall own the patent. The other party receives a non-exclusive license to use the patent for Mars NFT Land purposes.

---

## 6. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 6.1 Term

This agreement shall commence on the date of execution and continue for an initial term of **10 years** ("Initial Term"). At the end of the Initial Term, this agreement shall automatically renew for successive 5-year periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 180 days before the end of the then-current term.

### 6.2 Termination for Breach

Either party may terminate this agreement if the other party materially breaches any provision and fails to cure such breach within 60 days of written notice.

### 6.3 Termination for Convenience

Licensee may terminate this agreement for convenience with 180 days' written notice and payment of all accrued royalties through the termination date.

### 6.4 Survival

Sections 3 (Financial Terms), 4 (Audit Rights), 5 (Intellectual Property), and 8 (Confidentiality) shall survive termination of this agreement.

# ADVISORY AGREEMENT

## 1. ADVISORY SERVICES

### 1.1 Scope of Services

Advisor agrees to provide strategic advisory services to Company regarding the Mars NFT Land platform, including but not limited to:

**Quarterly Strategy Reviews:** Advisor shall participate in quarterly meetings (4 per year) with Company's leadership to review platform performance, market trends, and strategic direction. Each meeting shall last 2-4 hours.

**Market Analysis:** Advisor shall provide analysis of competitive developments, regulatory changes, and market opportunities in the Web3 and space economy sectors as they relate to Mars NFT Land.

**Partnership Development:** Advisor shall identify and introduce potential strategic partners, including space agencies (NASA, ESA), blockchain platforms, and media companies.

**Product Feedback:** Advisor shall provide feedback on product roadmap, user experience, and feature prioritization based on market insights and user feedback.

**Public Representation:** Advisor may represent Company at industry conferences, media interviews, and investor presentations as mutually agreed.

### 1.2 Time Commitment

Advisor shall dedicate approximately **8-10 hours per month** to advisory services, including:
- 2-4 hours for quarterly strategy reviews
- 2-3 hours for market analysis and research
- 2-3 hours for partnership development and communications
- Additional time for public representation as agreed

---

## 2. COMPENSATION

### 2.1 Monthly Retainer

Company shall pay Advisor a monthly retainer of **$75,000 USD** for advisory services, payable on the first business day of each month via wire transfer.

### 2.2 Additional Compensation

If Advisor is requested to perform services beyond the scope outlined in Section 1 (e.g., full-time consulting, extensive travel), Company shall negotiate additional compensation with Advisor in advance.

### 2.3 Equity Options

In addition to the monthly retainer, Company shall grant Advisor **options to purchase [X]% equity** in the Mars NFT Land entity, vesting over 4 years with a 1-year cliff. The strike price shall be determined based on an independent valuation at the time of grant.

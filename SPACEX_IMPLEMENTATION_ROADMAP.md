## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Technical Risks
- **Risk:** Smart contract vulnerability
- **Mitigation:** Multiple security audits, bug bounty program ($500K), insurance coverage

- **Risk:** Blockchain scalability issues
- **Mitigation:** Multi-chain deployment (Polygon, Ethereum, Arbitrum), Layer 2 solutions

- **Risk:** Oracle data feed failure
- **Mitigation:** Redundant oracle providers, manual override mechanism

### Market Risks
- **Risk:** Low user adoption
- **Mitigation:** SpaceX brand leverage, Starship timing, influencer partnerships

- **Risk:** Competitor entry
- **Mitigation:** First-mover advantage, exclusive SpaceX partnership, data moat

- **Risk:** Crypto market downturn
- **Mitigation:** Diversified revenue (data licensing, subscriptions), fiat payment options

### Regulatory Risks
- **Risk:** SEC classification as security
- **Mitigation:** Utility NFT structure, legal counsel, no-action letter request

- **Risk:** International regulatory issues
- **Mitigation:** Regional compliance teams, jurisdictional restrictions if needed

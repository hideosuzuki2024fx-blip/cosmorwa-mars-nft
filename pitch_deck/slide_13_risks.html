Risks & Mitigations

Addressing Key Challenges

Regulatory Risk
SEC classification as securities. International legal challenges.
Mitigation
Structure as utility NFTs (not securities). Early legal review. Compliance-first approach. Regulatory partnerships.
Technical Risk
Blockchain scalability. Data integrity. Oracle reliability.
Mitigation
Layer 2 solutions (Arbitrum, Polygon). Chainlink oracles. Redundant data sources. Extensive testing.
Adoption Risk
Low user adoption. Speculation vs. utility. Community fatigue.
Mitigation
Elon Musk endorsement. Celebrity partnerships. Educational content. Gamification. Community incentives.
Competitive Risk
Blue Origin or NASA launches similar project. First-mover advantage erodes.
Mitigation
Speed to market (Q2 2026). Network effects lock-in. Brand moat. Continuous innovation.

What Happens When Rovers Land
Rover Lands: Spacecraft touches down on Mars surface
Location Locked: Coordinates broadcast to blockchain
NFT Triggered: NFT owners at landing site receive notification
Event Recorded: Discovery event recorded on-chain
Value Update: NFT value dynamically updates based on discovery

  • Mineral discovery (high-value)
  • Water ice detection (critical)
  • Geological anomaly (medium)
